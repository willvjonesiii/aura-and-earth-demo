Who I Am

She is both soft and wild,
nurturing and fierce.

Within every woman lives a powerful, intuitive, and sensual force that has been forced to armor her heart and womb.

The Rose Goddess represents the feminine that is both soft and wild, nurturing and fierce, sensual and deeply wise. Like the rose, a woman carries beauty, depth, and thorns that protect her truth.

This temple is a space where women are invited to reconnect with that sacred energy within themselves.
